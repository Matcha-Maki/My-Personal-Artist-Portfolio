About Me

Hello, I'm KiraMae, an independent freelance digital concept artist and illustrator.

I am passionate about creating characters and stories through my art.

While I am partial to the digital art landscape, I am no stranger to 3D modeling and traditional oil paints.

I have an affinity for life around us, specifically the sea.

I hope that through my art and creativity we can all find just a little more beauty and joy in daily life that we often times miss in our busy lives.

There’s a little magic in life if you know where to look. ✨ Have courage, and be kind, always.
